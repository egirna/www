Partners

Egirna Technologies Limited, has been listed as a technology partner of VMRay Inc., the industry leader in dynamic malware analysis, delivering powerful agentless threat analysis tools and evasion-resistant solutions.

ICAPeg, an open source ICAP (Internet Content Adaptation Protocol) server, provided by Egirna Technologies, is a solution to integrate web gateways typically web proxy servers and firewalls which are ICAP protocol enabled, with VMRay Analyzer and Detector malware analysis and detection services, to protect your systems by inspecting files downloaded from the internet.
